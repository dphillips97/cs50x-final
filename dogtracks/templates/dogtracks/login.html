When you leave town, you want the best care for your pets. They prefer to stay in the place that they know best while you're away. Pet-sitters can help you keep your animals calm and happy, and you don't need to worry about picking up and dropping off your pets to a kennel.

Dogtracks allows you to create, add, delete, and view your pets and requst visits from a pet sitter. Once your request is submitted, the pet sitter will follow up with you.

As a pet-sitter, you can manage info about pets, visits, and pet owners. You can also confirm, complete, or cancel a requested visit.
